Professional Launch Privacy Policy

Professional Launch is designed for on-device use. It does not require an account, does not include cloud sync, and does not include advertising or analytics SDKs in this release.

What the app stores

  • Shell state and settings on device
  • Runtime product state on device
  • Exported files only when the user explicitly requests an export or share action

What the app does not do

  • No account creation
  • No remote database
  • No analytics SDK
  • No ad SDK
  • No background tracking

Support requests submitted through GitHub are subject to GitHub’s own policies.
