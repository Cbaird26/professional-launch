### Description

Professional Launch is the polished launch shell for the Theory of Everything Foundation app frame. ZoraASI is the in-app intelligence layer, and Zora is the engine that powers the runtime tabs inside the app.

The app combines guided comparison modes, route optimization, technical visualization, and research-facing controls in one grounded software interface. It is designed for exploratory modeling and open-science framing, not as a hardware claim or guaranteed predictor.

Included runtime surfaces:

- Probability Sculptor
- Fold-Space Engine
- Timeline Selector
- Decision
- Intent
- Navigation
- Autopilot
- Research
